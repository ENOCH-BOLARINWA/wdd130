Purpose

White water rafting is an adventurous and healthy way of exploring nature while diving deeper into the waters of the rivers. The fresh mountain air, a picturesque landscape, physical activity, and numerous rapids are no wonder why this sport is such healthy exercise.

Audience

Who are the target customers? They are people who loved healthy water exercise and are matured enough (above 21years) to sustain themselves financially. What do they want? They want an adventure that involves exploring through the use of raft boat. How will the audience access my site? They can access it from any computer devices.
